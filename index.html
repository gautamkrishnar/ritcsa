Computer Science Association
Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology


Computer Science Association (CSA) is a community of students and faculties of the computer science department of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of technology, Kottayam.

What we do?

Through regularly scheduled activities and meetings on computer science,
the CSA serves as a forum for networking, information sharing, idea exchange and problem solving for the computer science community in the college. It also have many sub communities to support student activities.
